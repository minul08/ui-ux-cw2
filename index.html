Who We are

Welcome to Book Mart, one of Sri Lanka's leading specialist bookstores. We're proud to offer more than 17 million titles at low prices -- all with free delivery islandwide. Whatever your interest or passion, you'll find something interesting in our bookshop full of delights.

Our Goal

Of the 30 million titles ever printed in the Sinhala,English and Tamil languages only a few million of these are in print. We seek to make available as many of these titles as possible (and working to do the same with foreign language titles). This way, we will have the largest breadth of titles available in the country.

Publishers & Distributors

We want to offer as many books as possible and we are very keen to talk to publishers and distributors about supply arrangements. Please do email us at procurements@bookmart.lk

eBooks

We do not sell eBooks on Book Mart yet,it is certainly a possibility that we will in the future.We do however sell thousands of physical books, so have a browse and enjoy!

Delivery

Dispatch times may vary for each item even if they are ordered at the same time.You'll get an email when your item has been dispatched so you can start making room on your shelves for your new books!
